SavingsFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Privacy-First Design: SavingsFlow stores all your financial data (savings goals, budgets, transactions, and calculations) locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your financial information on any external servers. Your data stays with you.

1. Who We Are

SavingsFlow is developed and operated by Pandemonium Software Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with UK GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

Data Controller: Pandemonium Software Ltd
Contact: pandemoniumsoftwareltd@gmail.com

2. Data We Store Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Financial Data: Savings goals, target amounts, current balances, budget categories, transactions, and projections
  • Security Data: Your app passcode (encrypted hash), recovery key (encrypted), and Face ID/Touch ID preferences
  • App Settings: Display preferences, notification settings, and feature toggles

Important: This data is stored using iOS Keychain (for security data) and standard app storage. If you delete the app or reset your device without backing up, this data is permanently lost. We cannot recover it for you.

3. Data Collected by Third-Party Services

While we don't collect your financial data, SavingsFlow uses third-party services that collect limited technical and usage information:

3.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

Purpose: Processing in-app purchases and managing subscriptions
Data Collected:

  • Anonymous user identifier (generated by RevenueCat)
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version

Legal Basis: Performance of contract (to provide premium features)
Data Location: United States (adequate safeguards in place)
Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

3.2 Google AdMob (Advertising)

Purpose: Displaying advertisements in the free version
Data Collected:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Ad Personalization: We use non-personalized ads by default. You can manage ad preferences in your device settings.
Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (to provide free app access)
Data Location: United States (adequate safeguards in place)
Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
Opt-Out: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings or upgrade to premium to remove ads entirely.

3.3 Expo Crash Reporting (Analytics)

Purpose: Detecting and fixing app crashes to improve stability
Data Collected:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Crash logs and error messages (technical data only)
  • Anonymous device identifier

Important: Crash reports do NOT include your financial data, passcodes, or personal information.
Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (to maintain app quality)
Privacy Policy: expo.dev/privacy

4. What We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you email us for support)
  • Bank account credentials or financial institution connections
  • Precise location data
  • Your app passcode or recovery key (stored encrypted on your device only)
  • Your biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID handled by iOS/Android, not us)
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

5. How We Use Data

The limited data collected by third-party services is used only for:

  • Subscription Management: Processing payments and verifying premium access
  • Advertising: Displaying relevant ads to free users
  • App Improvement: Fixing crashes and improving stability

We do NOT use data for:

  • Selling or sharing with data brokers
  • Building user profiles for marketing
  • Cross-app tracking or behavioral advertising (beyond standard AdMob)
  • Training AI models

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent necessary for app functionality.

We may disclose data if required by law, such as:

  • In response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud
  • In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition)

7. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Financial data (local) Until you delete the app or manually delete data
RevenueCat subscription data Duration of subscription + 1 year
AdMob advertising data Per Google's retention policy (typically 9-18 months)
Expo crash reports 90 days

8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK/EU user, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you (limited to third-party services)
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data from third-party services
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Portability: Export your local data using the app's export feature
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: Contact the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise these rights: Email us at pandemoniumsoftwareltd@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

SavingsFlow is rated 18+ and not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the app, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your local financial data never leaves your device. However, data collected by RevenueCat, AdMob, and Expo is transferred to and stored in the United States. These services comply with appropriate data transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses, UK-US Data Bridge).

11. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local Data: Encrypted using iOS Keychain and device encryption
  • Passcode: Stored as SHA256 hash, never plaintext
  • Third-Party Services: Use secure HTTPS connections
  • No Central Database: We don't operate servers that could be breached

Your Responsibility: Keep your device passcode, Face ID, and app passcode secure. Enable automatic backups so you don't lose data if your device is lost or stolen.

12. Backup and Data Export

You can export your local data at any time using the "Export Data" feature in Settings. This creates a JSON file you can save to:

  • iCloud Drive / Google Drive
  • Email to yourself
  • AirDrop to another device
  • Local device storage

Important: Backup files are NOT password-protected. Store them securely.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Updated "Last Modified" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
